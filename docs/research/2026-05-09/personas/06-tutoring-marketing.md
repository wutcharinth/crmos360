# Persona 06 — Marketing manager, tutoring centre, Bangkok

## Identity

**Name:** Wasin "Win" Sittiporn
**Age:** 29
**Role:** Marketing Manager
**Education:** BA Communication Arts, Bangkok University; ran a TikTok lifestyle channel as a side hustle, which is how he got into digital marketing.

## Company snapshot

**Brand:** "FutureMind Academy" — exam-prep tutoring (TGAT/TPAT, IGCSE, IB) for Thai high-schoolers, plus a math/science enrichment track for younger students. Hybrid: 3 physical centres + online live classes via Zoom.
**Location:** HQ + flagship in Sukhumvit 31; branches in Lat Phrao and Bangna.
**Headcount:** 25 staff (1 owner-principal + 14 tutors + 6 admin + 4 marketing/CS — Win runs 3 of those 4).
**Monthly revenue:** ~4.5M THB (~$128k USD); average term fee 28,000–55,000 THB.
**Channels:**
- **LINE OA** — primary lead-capture and parent-communication; 28,000 followers. ~400 msgs/day.
- **Facebook** — paid ads run by Win, lots of group activity (parents post about exam dates, Win monitors).
- **TikTok** — Win's wheelhouse; tutors do 15-second exam-tip videos. 80k followers, growing.
- **Phone** — older parents prefer to call; admin staff handle.
- **No Shopee/Lazada/IG DMs** for sales.
**Conversation volume:** ~400 LINE OA + Facebook DM /day combined. Heavy spikes in March (admissions) and Sep (mid-term).

## Current stack

- **LINE OA Premium** + **Manychat** Pro — Manychat does basic flows, hands off to admin staff.
- **Facebook Business Suite** for FB DMs.
- **Google Forms + Google Sheets** for student intake (parent name, child's school, target exam, schedule).
- **Slack** internal.
- **Zoom Pro** for online classes.
- **HubSpot Free** — Win signed up, never fully populated; abandoned after 6 weeks.
- No proper CRM. **Net spend on tools: ~9,000 THB/mo.**

## Day-in-the-life pain (Win's words, condensed)

> "Honestly the LINE OA is half lead-gen, half parent-management. The conversations split very differently:
>
> 1. **New leads** — parents asking about courses, schedules, prices. ~50% of inbound. We need to qualify them quickly and book a tour or trial class. Manychat handles this OK but the script is rigid; if a parent asks something off-flow ("does my son need TGAT or TPAT?"), it falls over.
>
> 2. **Existing parents** — homework questions, schedule changes, payment confirms. ~40%. Most of these should be self-service but aren't. They DM admin who has to dig into the spreadsheet.
>
> 3. **Spam / wrong-number / sticker-only messages** — ~10%. Annoying.
>
> What I want: AI that distinguishes "new parent inquiring" vs "existing parent asking" and routes accordingly. New = qualify and book. Existing = look up student, answer scheduling. The hard part: it needs to know our class catalog (which is in a Google Sheet I update weekly) and our student roster (also in Sheets).
>
> Also — I want it to help me with growth, not just service. Every week I see the same parent question: 'มีคอร์ส TGAT รอบใหม่เมื่อไหร่ครับ?' I should be turning that into a broadcast. If the AI told me 'this is the #1 question this week, want to send a campaign?', that's gold for marketing.
>
> Budget — we're a 4.5M THB/mo business, I have maybe 15–25k THB/mo for marketing tools. Currently spending 9k on LINE + Manychat + the dead HubSpot. Could reallocate."

## Budget authority

- **Decision-maker:** Win recommends, owner-principal (Khun Vichuda) approves >20,000 THB/mo. Win has discretion below 15k.
- **Comfort zone:** 8,000–25,000 THB/mo. Maybe 35k if it clearly replaces Manychat + does CRM.
- **Procurement style:** Self-serve, trial-led. Will sign up himself with the company card. Rarely takes sales calls.
- **Hard ceiling without proof:** 40,000 THB/mo.

## Decision criteria (ranked)

1. **Lead qualification flow that adapts** — not Manychat's rigid tree.
2. **Connects to Google Sheets** (class catalog + student roster). Can't replace these with another DB.
3. **Surfaces "what to do next" insights** to drive marketing campaigns.
4. **Mobile-first dashboard.** Win runs marketing from his phone half the time.
5. **Replaces Manychat at lower or same cost.**
6. **Handles Facebook DMs too**, not just LINE.

## Default objections (the things he reaches for first)

- *"ไม่อยาก migrate จาก Manychat ถ้าไม่ดีกว่าเยอะ"* — "Migration cost is real; need clearly better, not slightly better."
- *"ผูกกับ Google Sheets ได้มั้ย?"* — "Does it talk to Google Sheets? I'm not moving the catalog."
- *"AI ตอบเรื่องการศึกษาผิด ผู้ปกครองไม่พอใจแน่"* — "If AI gives wrong info to a parent — about exam dates, course content — they'll complain to the principal."
- *"Trial 14 วันได้มั้ย?"* — "Can I try it free for 14 days?"
- *"Facebook DM ใช้ได้มั้ย?"* — "Does it cover Facebook? I can't just have LINE."
